Strategy Tips

  • Double Damage: Play into counters to secure fast wins.
  • Frontline: Put evasive or tanky units (Thief, Knight) in front.
  • Backline: Keep damage dealers/support safe in the back.
  • Leader Choice: Leader affects unit capacity (engage cost).
  • Gear Priority: Favor items that grant extra actions or AP/PP.
  • Chase/Counter: Effects enabling pursuit/counterattacks are premium.
  • Promotion: Save Honor at C, promote at reputation B.
